Ideation

Prototype

How it works

• A "tree-like" lift network that connects to various key areas in the school.
• How commute from one place to another?
   1. Board the lift from one area.
   2. On the central platform, walk to another lift.
   3. Take another lift to the desired area.

Feedback

Positive
• Predictable. You know when the lift will come unlike waiting for a shuttle bus.
• Safer. No reckless driving, no sharp turns, no bumps. Just standing in a lift.

Negative
• Hard to get on during peak hours.
