EQUIPMe

Gym Equipment Classifier

This mini project aims to help beginner gymmers identify the various machines found at the gym!
You can take a picture with your phone's back camera using the button below and the predictions will be displayed beneath.

Model Loading...

EQUIPMe is a CNN classifier that uses transfer learning from the VGG16 model trained on imagenet dataset to regcognize different categories of gym equipment. It is currently able to detect 8 different categories of gym equipment using images sourced from the web. However, though the validation accuracy reached 99%, the online images might not be representative of real-life images.
